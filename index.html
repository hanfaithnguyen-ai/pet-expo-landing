The Ultimate Weekend for Pet Lovers

Super Pet Expo is a high-energy weekend for pet lovers—shopping, shows, rescues, and experiences for dogs, cats, and everyone in between. Bring the family, meet amazing animals, and discover products you didn’t know you needed.

What is Super Pet Expo?

Super Pet Expo brings together pet owners, animal lovers, vendors, and rescue organizations for an all-in-one event experience. Explore new products, enjoy live activities, and connect with people who care about animals as much as you do.
